Hankai House

The Hankai House System creates a strong sense of community, identity and friendly student competition. Each house represents a unique set of values, empowering students to grow in all aspects of life—academically, socially, creatively and physically. Students participate in inter-house competitions throughout the year, including academic challenges, sports tournaments, cultural celebrations and service projects, fostering collaboration, leadership and school spirit.

Odin

Values: Leadership | Knowledge | Resilience

Odin House takes its name from the Norse god of wisdom and strategy, symbolising intellectual strength, leadership and perseverance. Students of Odin are known for their academic excellence, problem‑solving skills and leadership roles in the classroom and extracurricular activities. Whether it’s excelling in STEM competitions, leading school‑wide initiatives or achieving top honours, Odin House fosters a community of curious learners and determined leaders.

Motto: “Lead with Wisdom, Triumph with Strength.”

Awilix

Values: Creativity | Ambition | Innovation

Awilix House is named after the Mayan goddess of the moon, symbolising creative energy, ambition and ingenuity. This house nurtures a passion for the arts, literature and design, with its students shining in creative pursuits, performances and artistic innovation. Awilix inspires its members to dream big, think outside the box and push academic and extracurricular boundaries.

Motto: “Create Fearlessly, Shine Brightly.”

Athena

Values: Wisdom | Health | Teamwork

Athena House, named after the Greek goddess of wisdom and strategy, emphasises a balanced approach to learning and life. Known for fostering academic achievement, physical well‑being and teamwork, Athena students excel in sports, debates and collaborative projects. With a strong focus on holistic growth, Athena encourages its members to build a sound mind in a healthy body, ready to face challenges with confidence and grace.

Motto: “Strive Together, Excel Together.”

Ra

Values: Energy | Determination | Courage

Ra House takes its name from the Egyptian sun god, representing energy, strength and determination. Students in Ra House are champions of sportsmanship, resilience and adventurous spirit, consistently performing in athletics, outdoor activities and team challenges. Ra inspires its members to face adversity, develop unwavering confidence and shine brightly in all pursuits.

Motto: “Ignite the Spirit, Embrace the Challenge.”
